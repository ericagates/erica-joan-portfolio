About

I'm a California girl, born and raised in Huntington Beach but I now live in sunny San Diego! I graduated from UC San Diego with a degree in Structural Engineering and after working for several years as a civil engineer, I decided it was time for a new career in the exciting tech field. Being a web developer and visual designer has been the most fun and fufilling work I've done so far! I just love the creativity and seeing ideas become a reality. When I'm not working, you can find me scuba diving, traveling (I speak fluent Japanese), line dancing, and working out at my local aerial arts/pole dance studio. I can't wait to help design and develop your brand vision!
